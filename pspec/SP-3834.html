The native desktop app integrates Podium top-bar content into the operating-system title-bar area. On macOS, the PODIUM logo appears to the right of the traffic-light controls and replaces the plain window title, followed by the rest of the app top bar. The traffic-light controls are vertically centered in the integrated header, and the horizontal gap from the green control to the logo matches the gap from the logo to the Home link. The integration must account for desktop platform differences, preserve theme-aware backgrounds, and does not apply to the web app. The desktop application icon is derived from the Podium logo.
